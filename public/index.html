🐾 Did You Know?

Fascinating facts about our feline friends

😴

Cats Sleep A LOT

Cats sleep between 12 to 16 hours a day. They are crepuscular, meaning most active at dawn and dusk.

👂

Super Hearing

Cats can hear frequencies up to 64kHz — compared to humans who top out at around 20kHz.

🐾

Unique Nose Prints

Just like human fingerprints, every cat's nose print is completely unique!

💨

Speed Runners

Domestic cats can sprint up to 30 mph (48 km/h) over short distances.

🗣️

Meowing for Humans

Adult cats rarely meow at each other — they developed meowing specifically to communicate with humans!

🌙

Night Vision

Cats can see in near darkness. Their eyes need about 6x less light than humans to see clearly.
